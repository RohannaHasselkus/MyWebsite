Artist Bio

My name is Rohanna Hasselkus and I am currently a Junior at Denver School of the Arts HS. I am interested Visual arts due to the way it can be applied to effectively communicate a broad range of concepts through a universal language. As someone who is multilingual (additional spoken languages include Spanish, French and German) and indentifies as multiple nationalities, the idea of communication through the medium of art has always interested me. By employing the affinity that humanity has to understand and interpret visual information, visual arts can be an important tool to make complicated ideas and processes more widely accessible. This is important due to the contemporary methods in which information, regardless of accuracy is rapidly spread on social platforms. I want to use art as a weapon to educate and combat misinformation. Beyond the field of visual arts, I am interested in mycology, the study of fungi.
